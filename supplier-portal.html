Non-Negotiable Standards

To ensure EU marketability and safety, all potential partners must meet these mandatory requirements

✓
Organic & Bio-Compliance (Regulation EU 2018/848) Certification must be issued by an EU-recognized body listed in Annex II of Regulation EU 2021/2325. For India, this must explicitly cover Category D (Processed Products).
✓
TRACES NT & COI Commitment Partners must be registered in TRACES NT. You must commit to issuing and digitally signing the Certificate of Inspection (COI) in Box 18 before the shipment leaves India.
✓
Cosmetic GMP (ISO 22716) Manufacturing according to ISO 22716 (Cosmetic GMP) is mandatory to ensure product safety for our industrial clients.
✓
Bakuchiol High-Purity Isolate Minimum purity of >99% (HPLC) is required. Maximum level of Furocoumarins (Psoralens) must be < 50 ppm to comply with EU safety limits.
✓
REACH & Extraction Standards For Moringa and Lemongrass oil, confirmation of "Physical Extraction only" (Cold Pressed / Steam Distilled) is required to ensure REACH exemption under Annex V.
✓
Ethical & Social Compliance Active SA8000 or SEDEX membership is preferred to comply with the German Supply Chain Due Diligence Act (LkSG).

Mandatory Technical Documentation

"The PIF-Ready Package" – We require a complete regulatory dossier for every batch. Your application is only valid if you can provide:

01

Batch-specific CoA

Detailed chemical fingerprint including density, refractive index, and purity levels.

02

Advanced Allergen Statement

A precise breakdown of all EU-regulated allergens according to Regulation (EU) 2023/1545 (80+ substances).

03

MSDS / Technical Data Sheet

Provision of your available Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS). This serves as the technical basis for our internal conversion into EU-REACH compliant formats.

04

Animal Testing Ban Declaration

Formal confirmation that no animal testing has been performed (Art. 18, Regulation EU 1223/2009).
